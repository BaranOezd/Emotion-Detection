Harry looked up at Hagrid, his mouth open. “Everyone knows yer the famous Harry Potter,” said Hagrid. “Why, I’d bet my las’ Knut that Dumbledore left yeh the letter with yer aunt an’ uncle, hopin’ they’d tell yeh. Yeh’ve heard o’ Hogwarts, o’ course?” “Er — no,” said Harry. Hagrid looked shocked. “Sorry,” Harry added quickly.
